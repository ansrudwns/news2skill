Activate ONLY for tasks involving heavy cryptology, algebraic proofs, or hackathon challenges.

2. **Symbolic Verification:** Before finalizing an answer or algorithm, you MUST translate the core logic into a rigid symbolic or formal constraint verification step (akin to a structural Assert statement).
